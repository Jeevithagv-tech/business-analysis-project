# Cafe Express – Online Ordering & Queue Management System

This repository contains an end-to-end Business Analysis case study focused on improving customer experience and operational efficiency in a café environment.

The project addresses a real-world business problem where manual order taking during peak hours leads to long queues, increased waiting times, and reduced customer satisfaction.

The proposed solution is a web-based ordering system that enables customers to place and customize orders digitally, reducing service delays and improving order accuracy.

## Business Problem

During peak operating hours, café staff spend approximately 5–6 minutes per customer capturing order details, including drink preferences, customizations, allergy information, payment methods, and receipt options.

This results in:

- Long customer queues
- Increased waiting times
- Higher risk of order errors
- Reduced operational efficiency
- Lower customer satisfaction
